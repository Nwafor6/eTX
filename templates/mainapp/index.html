About eTX

eTX is an application aimed to easy the stress of transcript computation for student both in tertiatary institutions and basic and secondary schools. eTX however provides a stressfree work for the exam officer of the institution.

The application requires the upload of academic documents such as list of students and exam score records. Each student's transcript can be downloaded as a PDF file and printed.
